## 핵심 요약

Recent GPU generations deliver significantly higher FLOPs using lower-precision arithmetic, such as FP8. While successfully applied to large language models (LLMs), its adoption in large recommendation models (LRMs) has been limited. This is because LRMs are numerically sensitive, dominated by small matrix multiplications (GEMMs) followed by normalization, and trained in communication-intensive environments.
