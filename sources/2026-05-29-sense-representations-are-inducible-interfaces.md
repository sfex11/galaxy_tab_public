## 핵심 요약

Sense representations (explicit, per-token meaning decompositions) are useful for disambiguation, steering, and cross-lingual alignment, but existing approaches require models to be pretrained with sense structure baked in. We introduce ACROS, which induces an explicit sense pathway into a frozen pretrained decoder LM through a gated residual addition.
