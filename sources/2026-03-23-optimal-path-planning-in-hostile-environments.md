## 핵심 요약

Coordinating agents through hazardous environments, such as aid-delivering drones navigating conflict zones or field robots traversing deployment areas filled with obstacles, poses fundamental planning challenges. We introduce and analyze the computational complexity of a new multi-agent path planning problem that captures this setting.
