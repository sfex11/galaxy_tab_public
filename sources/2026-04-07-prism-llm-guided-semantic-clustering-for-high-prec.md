# PRISM: LLM-Guided Semantic Clustering for High-Precision Topics

## 핵심 요약

In this paper, we propose Precision-Informed Semantic Modeling (PRISM), a structured topic modeling framework combining the benefits of rich representations captured by LLMs with the low cost and interpretability of latent semantic clustering methods. PRISM fine-tunes a sentence encoding model using a sparse set of LLM- provided labels on samples drawn from some corpus of interest.
