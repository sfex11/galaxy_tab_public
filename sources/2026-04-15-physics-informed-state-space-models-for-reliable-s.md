## 핵심 요약

The stable operation of autonomous off-grid photovoltaic systems dictates reliance on solar forecasting algorithms that respect atmospheric thermodynamics. Contemporary deep learning models consistently exhibit critical anomalies, primarily severe temporal phase lags during cloud transients and physically impossible nocturnal power generation.
