## 핵심 요약

Problem posing is a valuable learning activity in computing education, encouraging learners to actively construct, refine, and reflect on problems rather than simply solving them. This experience report presents the design and pilot deployment of two LLM-powered scaffolding systems for supporting problem posing across two computational scenarios with different levels of task openness.
