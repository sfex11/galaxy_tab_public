# QRAFTI: An Agentic Framework for Empirical Research in Quantitative Finance

## 핵심 요약

We introduce a multi-agent framework intended to emulate parts of a quantitative research team and support equity factor research on large financial panel datasets. QRAFTI integrates a research toolkit for panel data with MCP servers that expose data access, factor construction, and custom coding operations as callable tools.
