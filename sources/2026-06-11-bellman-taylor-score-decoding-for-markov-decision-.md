## 핵심 요약

Many Markov decision processes (MDPs) in operations research have feasible actions that are state dependent and defined implicitly by various operational constraints. These features make it difficult to use standard deep reinforcement learning (DRL) algorithms, whose action interfaces typically assume either a fixed finite action catalog or a simple Euclidean space.
