## 핵심 요약

Large language model (LLM)-based generative list-wise recommendation has advanced rapidly, but decoding remains sequential and thus latency-prone. To accelerate inference without changing the target distribution, speculative decoding (SD) uses a small draft model to propose several next tokens at once and a target LLM to verify and accept the longest prefix, skipping multiple steps per round.
