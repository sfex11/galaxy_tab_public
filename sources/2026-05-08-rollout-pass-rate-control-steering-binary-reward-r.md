# Rollout Pass-Rate Control: Steering Binary-Reward RL Toward Its Most Informative Regime

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-05-08

## 핵심 요약

SWE-bench-style agentic reinforcement learning relies on expensive stateful trajectories, yet substantial compute is wasted on sampled rollout groups with skewed pass rates, where binary rewards provide a weak contrastive signal.
