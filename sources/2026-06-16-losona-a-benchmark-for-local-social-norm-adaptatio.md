# LoSoNA: A Benchmark for Local Social Norm Adaptation in Group Conversations

**타입**: 논문

## 핵심 요약

Online group chats are social spaces with local conversational norms that are rarely stated explicitly. The ability and willingness of LLM-based agents to recognize and adapt to these norms remains mostly unexplored. We introduce LoSoNA, a benchmark for local social norm adaptation in multi-party chat.
