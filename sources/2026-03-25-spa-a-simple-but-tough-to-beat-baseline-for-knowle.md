# SPA: A Simple but Tough-to-Beat Baseline for Knowledge Injection

## 핵심 요약

While large language models (LLMs) are pretrained on massive amounts of data, their knowledge coverage remains incomplete in specialized, data-scarce domains, motivating extensive efforts to study synthetic data generation for knowledge injection. We propose SPA (Scaling Prompt-engineered Augmentation), a simple but tough-to-beat baseline that uses a small set of carefully designed prompts to generate large-scale synthetic data for knowledge injection.
