# Interfacial optical absorptance of air-water interfaces

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-03-19  
**링크**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17977v1

## 핵심 요약

The photomolecular effect has been hypothesized to enhance evaporation of water at visible wavelengths. This study develops a measurement technique to investigate its presence and magnitude at the liquid-vapor interface of water. The experiment detects surface absorptance by comparing polarized reflectance for substrates with and without a water layer.
