# TriViewBench: Controlled Complexity Scaling for Multi-View Structural Reasoning in MLLMs

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-06-26

## 핵심 요약

Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) demonstrate strong performance on standard visual question answering benchmarks, yet their scalability under controlled structural complexity remains poorly understood. We introduce TriViewBench, a controlled three-view visual reasoning benchmark constructed from synthetic 3D scenes with explicitly parameterized object count and occlusion.
