## 핵심 요약

Supervised fine-tuning (SFT) induces new behaviors in large language models, yet imposes no structural constraint on how these behaviors are distributed within the model. Existing behavior interpretation methods, such as circuit attribution approaches, identify sparse subnetworks correlated with SFT-induced behaviors post-hoc.
