## 핵심 요약

There is an increasing need for young people to become critically AI literate, understanding not only how AI works but also its limitations and ethical nuances. Yet, designing learning experiences that make such complex, serious topics engaging remains a challenge. This paper explores transformational games as a promising approach for supporting youth learning about generative AI (GenAI) and ethics.
