## 핵심 요약

Vision-language models (VLMs) have shown impressive capabilities in perceptual tasks, yet they degrade in complex multi-hop reasoning under multiplayer game settings with imperfect and deceptive information. In this paper, we study a representative multiplayer task, Murder Mystery Games, which require inferring hidden truths based on partial clues provided by roles with different intentions.
