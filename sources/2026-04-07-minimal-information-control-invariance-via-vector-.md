## 핵심 요약

Safety-critical autonomous systems must satisfy hard state constraints under tight computational and sensing budgets, yet learning-based controllers are often far more complex than safe operation requires. To formalize this gap, we study how many distinct control signals are needed to render a compact set forward invariant under sampled-data control, connecting the question to the information-theoretic notion of invariance entropy.
