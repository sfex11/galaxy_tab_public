# Stochastic Multi-Objective Kinodynamic Planning Against Adversaries

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-07-23  
**링크**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19284v1

## 핵심 요약

This paper addresses multi-objective kinodynamic planning in environments with stochastic hybrid adversaries that probabilistically transition to adversarial modes based on the ego state. The goal is to construct the Pareto-front of paths that trade off execution cost and the probability of safety constraint violation (risk).
