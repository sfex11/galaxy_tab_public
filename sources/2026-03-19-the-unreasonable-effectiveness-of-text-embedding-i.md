# The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Text Embedding Interpolation for Continuous Image Steering

## 핵심 요약

We present a training-free framework for continuous and controllable image editing at test time for text-conditioned generative models. In contrast to prior approaches that rely on additional training or manual user intervention, we find that a simple steering in the text-embedding space is sufficient to produce smooth edit control.
