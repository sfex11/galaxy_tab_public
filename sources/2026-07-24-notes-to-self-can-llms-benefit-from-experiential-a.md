## 핵심 요약

Humans distill experience into reusable abstractions, e.g., strategies and cautionary reminders, and apply them to gradually solve problems more effectively. We study whether Large Language Models (LLMs) can similarly benefit from such experiential abstractions. From LLMs' solution traces on the MATH training set, a stronger teacher or the LLMs themselves extract natural-language abstractions into a retrievable library.
