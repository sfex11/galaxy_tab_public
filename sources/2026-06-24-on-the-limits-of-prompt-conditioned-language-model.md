# On the Limits of Prompt-Conditioned Language Models as General-Purpose Learners

## 핵심 요약

Large Language Models (LLMs) are frequently portrayed as general-purpose solvers capable of solving arbitrary tasks. We argue that this view overlooks a fundamental constraint: language is a compressed and capacity-limited interface for conveying task information.
