## 핵심 요약

This work studies resilient output containment for heterogeneous linear multi-agent systems with actuator cyber-attacks over directed network topologies. The leaders generate bounded locally absolutely continuous trajectories; however, their dynamics, velocity bounds, and motion envelopes are undisclosed to the followers.
