## 핵심 요약

Discourse particles, such as \textit{well} and \textit{kind of}, are crucial components that enable LLMs to ``speak'' more like humans. They are used to convey emotions, intentions, and interpersonal meanings. However, existing studies have not yet built a comprehensive understanding of LLMs' capabilities in handling discourse particles.
