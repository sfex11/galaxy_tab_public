## 핵심 요약

Deep reinforcement learning has recently achieved strong results in quadrupedal locomotion, yet policies trained in simulation often fail to transfer when the environment changes. Evolutionary reinforcement learning aims to address this limitation by combining gradient-based policy optimisation with population-driven exploration.
