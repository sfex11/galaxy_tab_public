# TiCo: Time-Controllable Training for Spoken Dialogue Models

**타입**: 논문

## 핵심 요약

We propose TiCo, a simple post-training method for enabling spoken dialogue models (SDMs) to follow time-constrained instructions and generate responses with controllable duration. This capability is valuable for real-world spoken language systems such as voice assistants and interactive agents, where controlling response duration can improve interaction quality.
