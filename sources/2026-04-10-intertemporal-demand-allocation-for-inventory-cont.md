## 핵심 요약

Online marketplaces increasingly do more than simply match buyers and sellers: they route orders across competing sellers and, in many categories, offer ancillary fulfillment services that make seller inventory a source of platform revenue. We investigate how a platform can use intertemporal demand allocation to influence sellers' inventory choices without directly controlling stock.
