## 핵심 요약

We present AHOY, a method for reconstructing complete, animatable 3D Gaussian avatars from in-the-wild monocular video despite heavy occlusion. Existing methods assume unoccluded input-a fully visible subject, often in a canonical pose-excluding the vast majority of real-world footage where people are routinely occluded by furniture, objects, or other people.
