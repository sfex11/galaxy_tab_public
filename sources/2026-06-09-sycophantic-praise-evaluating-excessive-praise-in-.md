# Sycophantic Praise: Evaluating Excessive Praise in Language Models

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-06-09  
**링크**: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07441v1

## 핵심 요약

Sycophancy in language models is typically studied as excessive agreement or validation, while explicit praise and flattery have received comparatively little attention. We argue that sycophantic praise is a distinct alignment problem that cannot be reliably measured using current methods. We introduce a parameterized framework that measures whether praise is excessive relative to contribution quality and expected user ability.
