# Contagion Networks: Evaluator Bias Propagation in Multi-Agent LLM Systems

**타입**: 논문

## 핵심 요약

When large language models serve as evaluators in multi-agent systems, their systematic evaluation biases propagate through the agent network. We introduce Contagion Networks, a formal framework for measuring how evaluator biases spread across interacting LLM agents.
