# Vision-OPD: Learning to See Fine Details for Multimodal LLMs via On-Policy Self-Distillation

**타입**: 논문  
**출처**: arXiv  
**날짜**: 2026-05-20

## 핵심 요약

Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) still struggle with fine-grained visual understanding, where answers often depend on small but decisive evidence in the full image. We observe a regional-to-global perception gap: the same MLLM answers fine-grained questions more accurately when conditioned on evidence-centered crops than on the corresponding full images, suggesting that many failures stem from difficulty to focus on relevant evidence rather than insufficient local recognition ability.
